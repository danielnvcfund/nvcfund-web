Payment Options for NVCT Accounts

How This Works: You can fund your NVCT account using various payment methods. Select a payment option below to get started.
Credit Card Payment

Fund your NVCT account securely using a credit or debit card. Instant processing with receipts.

  • Instant funding to your NVCT account
  • Secure processing via Stripe
  • Automatic receipts by email
  • 3.5% processing fee
Pay with Credit Card
PayPal Payment

Use your PayPal account to fund your NVCT account securely and conveniently.

  • Pay with your PayPal balance or linked accounts
  • Secure payment processing
  • Usually processed within 24 hours
  • 3.0% processing fee
Pay with Card
Wire Transfer

Fund your NVCT account via domestic or international wire transfer from your bank.

  • Suitable for larger deposit amounts
  • Processing time: 2-5 business days
  • Detailed tracking of your wire transfer
  • No processing fee (bank fees may apply)
Wire Transfer
ACH Transfer (US Only)

Fund your NVCT account via ACH transfer from a US bank account.

  • US bank accounts only
  • Processing time: 1-3 business days
  • Recurring funding option available
  • No processing fee
ACH Transfer
Funding Information:
  • For tax compliance purposes, all funding sources may require KYC information
  • NVCT accounts are funded in USD equivalent (1 NVCT = 1 USD)
  • Processing times are estimates and may vary based on financial institutions
  • For amounts over $100,000, please contact your account manager directly
