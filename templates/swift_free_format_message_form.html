About SWIFT MT799 Messages

SWIFT MT799 is a free format text message that allows financial institutions to communicate with one another in a secure, standardized format. This message type is commonly used for:

  • General banking communications
  • Clarifying instructions or providing additional information
  • Pre-advice for financial transactions
  • Queries related to other SWIFT messages

SWIFT MT799 Message Format

Common Uses
  • Request clarification on prior transactions
  • Provide additional information about transfers
  • Send general banking communications
  • Reply to queries about other SWIFT messages
  • Pre-advise upcoming financial transactions
Best Practices
  • Be clear and concise in your message
  • Always include relevant reference numbers
  • Specify the transaction or topic you're addressing
  • Keep sensitive information to a minimum
  • Follow your institution's communication policies

Note: The recipient will typically respond within 1-2 business days
