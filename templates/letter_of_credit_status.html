About SWIFT MT760 Messages

The MT760 message is a standardized SWIFT message type used for Standby Letters of Credit (SBLC). These financial instruments serve as a guarantee of payment, ensuring that financial obligations will be met if certain conditions are satisfied.

Key Information
  • SWIFT messages are securely transmitted through the SWIFT network connecting financial institutions worldwide.
  • Once delivered, the receiving bank will process the Letter of Credit according to their internal procedures.
  • Processing times may vary depending on the receiving institution.
  • Status updates are typically available within 24-48 hours of transmission.
