About SWIFT Fund Transfers

SWIFT is the global standard for secure financial messaging between banks worldwide. Using SWIFT MT103 and MT202 message types, you can send money to beneficiaries around the world.

  • MT103: For customer fund transfers to individuals or businesses
  • MT202: For financial institution transfers between banks

SWIFT Fund Transfer Process

MT103 Customer Transfer
  • Used for sending money to individual beneficiaries or businesses
  • Contains detailed information about the sender and the beneficiary
  • Typically settles within 1-3 business days
  • Provides clear details about the purpose of the payment
MT202 Financial Institution Transfer
  • Used for bank-to-bank transfers between financial institutions
  • Faster settlement times than customer transfers
  • Less detailed, as it's between financial professionals
  • Used for correspondent banking and treasury operations

Note: Transfer fees may apply based on your agreement with the receiving institution
