SWIFT Fund Transfer Types

MT103: Customer Credit Transfer
  • Used for sending payments from/to customer accounts
  • Contains detailed customer information
  • Most common SWIFT message type for international payments
  • Processing typically takes 1-3 business days
MT202: Financial Institution Transfer
  • Used for bank-to-bank transfers
  • Used for cover payments and treasury operations
  • Contains less customer information than MT103
  • Typically processes faster than MT103

Note: Standard fees apply for all SWIFT fund transfers
