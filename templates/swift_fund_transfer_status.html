About SWIFT Fund Transfers

MT103 Customer Credit Transfer

MT103 is the SWIFT message format used for customer credit transfers. It's the most commonly used message for international wire transfers for a customer.

  • Contains detailed information about the sender (ordering customer)
  • Contains detailed information about the recipient (beneficiary customer)
  • Typically takes 1-3 business days to settle
  • Used primarily for individual and business payments
MT202 Financial Institution Transfer

MT202 is the SWIFT message format used for financial institution transfers. It's used for bank-to-bank transfers.

  • Simpler format than MT103, with less customer information
  • Typically settles faster than customer transfers
  • Used for correspondent banking relationships
  • Common for treasury operations and nostro account movements
Note: Fund transfers through SWIFT are irrevocable once the status changes to "Settled". Contact customer support if you have any questions about your transfer.
